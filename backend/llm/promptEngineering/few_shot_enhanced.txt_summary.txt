#### Cigdem Sengul

**Summary:**

Cigdem Sengul is a distinguished researcher and educator in the field of computer networks, currently serving as a Senior Lecturer at Brunel University. With over a decade of experience in both academia and industry, her expertise spans a wide range of areas within wireless networks, including network protocols, energy efficiency, interference management, and the Internet of Things (IoT). 

**Academic and Professional Background:**

Cigdem Sengul has a rich academic and professional background. She has held significant positions in various institutions and organizations:
- **Brunel University**: Senior Lecturer, focusing on network privacy, security, identity, and authorization.
- **Nominet**: Senior Researcher (2015-2019), where she worked on standards for building privacy and trust in the IoT.
- **Oxford Brookes University**: Senior Lecturer (2012-2015), where she lectured and conducted research on wireless and mobile networks.
- **Telekom Innovation Labs**: Senior Research Scientist (2008-2012), leading projects on Wireless Mesh Networks.

Cigdem has been recognized as a Fulbright fellow, a Department of Computer Science fellow at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), and a Vodafone fellow.

**Research Contributions:**

Cigdem Sengul has made significant contributions to the field of wireless networks, with her work being published in over 50 journal and conference publications. Some of her notable publications include:
- "Forward and Backhaul Link Optimization for Energy Efficient OFDMA Small Cell Networks" (2017)
- "Load-adaptive networking for energy-efficient wireless access" (2015)
- "Measurement-based simulation of WiFi interference" (2013)
- "Energino: energy saving tips for your wireless network" (2012)
- "A survey of adaptive services to cope with dynamics in wireless self-organizing networks" (2012)

Her research often focuses on improving energy efficiency, optimizing network performance, and enhancing security and privacy in wireless networks.

**Advocacy and Outreach:**

Cigdem is a passionate advocate for diversity in STEM fields. She serves as the Communications Co-Chair and Secretary of ACM-W Global, an organization dedicated to supporting women in computing. She has also been involved with ACM Women-Europe as the Communication and Outreach Chair (2019-2022). Additionally, she collaborates with the Micro:bit Educational Foundation to promote coding education among school children and is a co-author of the book "Networking with the Micro:bit."
